Lasagna recipe

Description

Odin's favorite food. He could eat it for every meal.

Although usually made with meat, there is a vegeterian alternative.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Meat sauce
  • Beschamel sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Put the pasta on the bottom of the pan
  2. Add meat sauce
  3. Add beschamel sauce
  4. Add more pasta
  5. Repeat stpes 2 to 5 untill you run out of ingredeants
  6. Let it rest for an hour or two, and warm up the oven
  7. Put the lasagna in hot oven
  8. When baked, add parmesan
