Pizza recipe

Description

Hunter's pizza is widely known across the universe. One of the finest and simplest examples of Asgardian cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded cheese
  • Picant salami
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Warm the oven
  2. Spread the dough
  3. Spread the sauce on the dough
  4. Add cheese on top
  5. Add salami
  6. Put pizza in the oven
  7. When it's almost done, add the egg and bake it untill it's done
