Soup recipe

Description

Tasty chicken noodle soup made from finest asgardian chikens.

Historically, the soup was made with rice balls instead of noodles, but modern cooks decided to use noodles instead. However, some cooks still use the old, so called, Odin's recipe, and use rice balls.

Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Chicken meat and bones
  • Water
  • Assorted vegetables

Steps:

  1. Put the water to boil
  2. Clean the vegetables
  3. Put the vegetablesand chicken in the water to cook
  4. When all is cooked, drain the soup in another pot
  5. Put the noodles in the soup
  6. While noodles cook, clean the meat from the bones and add it to the soup
  7. Add spices as you wish
